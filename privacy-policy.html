1) Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to operate and improve the App:

  • Account Information: your email address and authentication identifiers when you sign up or sign in.
  • Financial Data You Enter: values you choose to track in the App (e.g., account balances, asset values, notes). You control what you add or remove.
  • Device & Diagnostics: device model, OS, app version, crash reports, and performance data (to identify problems and improve reliability).
  • Usage Data: interactions with screens and features to help us understand what works and improve the experience.

2) How We Use Your Data

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • Debug, prevent, and investigate errors, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Personalize in-app experience and product communications.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3) Data Storage & Retention

You can delete your account and all associated personal data directly from within the App (Account → Delete Account). If you encounter any issues, you can also contact us at worthtrackerapp@gmail.com. We will take reasonable steps to honor your request, subject to any legal retention requirements.

4) Third-Party Services

We rely on reputable providers to operate app functionality, analytics, authentication, subscriptions, storage, and crash reporting. These services may collect information used to identify you. Please review their policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database
  • RevenueCat

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers under agreements that limit their use to operating the App on our behalf.

5) Cookies

The App itself does not set cookies in the mobile runtime. However, third-party SDKs or our website may use cookies or similar technologies to operate and improve their services. You can control cookies through your device or browser settings.

6) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7) Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA and similar)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise your rights, contact us at worthtrackerapp@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.
